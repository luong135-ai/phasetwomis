ABOUT

What is Phoholic?

At Pho Holic, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a gathering place where everyone feels like family. When you step through our doors, you're not just a customer, you're a friend joining us for a taste of authentic Vietnamese warmth. Our pho, crafted with love and tradition, tells a story in every spoonful, connecting you to generations of flavor and hospitality. Come on in, pull up a chair, and let us share a little piece of our home with yours.

Our Story

Phở Holic Duluth is your new destination for a modern and elevated Vietnamese dining experience. Inspired by a journey of culinary refinement, we offer an exquisite menu that expertly blends traditional Vietnamese flavors with creative, contemporary twists.

MODERN AMBIANCE, PREMIUM EXPERIENCE Step into our modern, sophisticated, and spacious environment. Designed for comfort and style, Phở Holic is the perfect setting for a memorable family dinner, a lively gathering with friends, or any special occasion.

INSPIRED CUISINE, FRESH INGREDIENTS Our menu is a passionate blend of authentic Vietnamese dishes, crafted by talented chefs using only the freshest, cleanest, and highest-quality ingredients. We transform every meal—from a comforting bowl of phở to our unique creations—into a culinary art piece that engages both the palate and the eye.

PROFESSIONAL SERVICE We are committed to providing professional, warm, and attentive service. From the moment you place your order to your final bite, our dedicated team ensures your dining experience is smooth, fast, and entirely satisfying.
